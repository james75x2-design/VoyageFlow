# VoyageFlow Booking Policies and Guarantees

## What VoyageFlow Can Guarantee

VoyageFlow guarantees the following:

- Itineraries are generated using its curated destination knowledge base
- Booking links are correctly formatted and point to legitimate partner platforms
- Trip context (dates, destinations, traveler count) is passed accurately to partner search pages
- All recommendations are grounded in the VoyageFlow knowledge base with visible citations

## What VoyageFlow Cannot Guarantee

VoyageFlow **cannot** guarantee:

- **Hotel availability** at the time of booking — inventory changes minute to minute on partner platforms
- **Hotel prices** — rates fluctuate based on demand, promotions, and partner platform dynamics
- **Flight availability or fares** — airline pricing is dynamic and not stored inside VoyageFlow
- **Cancellation or refund outcomes** — these are governed by the partner platform's terms, not by VoyageFlow
- **Visa approvals, entry permissions, or health clearances** — these are the traveler's responsibility

Users must confirm all pricing, availability, and policies directly with the booking partner before completing payment.

## Verifying Information Before Booking

VoyageFlow strongly encourages users to verify the following before finalizing any booking:

- Current price on the partner platform (VoyageFlow does not display live prices)
- Cancellation policy and refund window
- Room type, bed configuration, and included amenities
- Total cost including taxes, fees, and resort charges
- Passport validity and visa requirements for the destination

## Out-of-Scope Topics

VoyageFlow does not provide:

- Medical advice for travel — users should consult a licensed physician or travel medicine clinic
- Legal advice about visas, immigration, or customs
- Real-time weather forecasts or emergency alerts
- Personal financial or insurance recommendations

For these topics, VoyageFlow directs users to appropriate licensed professionals or official government resources.
